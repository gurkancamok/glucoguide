# GlucoGuide Clinical

A digital clinical support system designed to improve diabetes medication safety, standardize patient education, and provide accessible, real-time medication guidance.

---

## 🔍 Overview

GlucoGuide Clinical is a web-based platform developed to address a common clinical gap:
the lack of accessible, standardized, and patient-friendly medication information for individuals with diabetes.

In many clinical settings, patient education relies on static printed forms, which are difficult to update, not always accessible, and often insufficient for safe medication use.

This project transforms that process into a dynamic, digital, and accessible system.

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

* 📱 **QR Code-Based Access**
  Patients can instantly access medication information by scanning QR codes.

* 💊 **Medication-Specific Guidance**
  Each drug includes dosage instructions, usage timing, and side effects.

* ⚠️ **Patient Safety Alerts**
  Critical warnings (e.g., hypoglycemia risk) are clearly highlighted.

* 🌐 **Web-Based & Mobile Compatible**
  Accessible anytime via smartphone, tablet, or desktop.

* 🔄 **Standardized Patient Education**
  Eliminates variability in how patients receive medication information.

---

## 🏥 Clinical Impact

This system aims to:

* Reduce medication errors
* Improve patient understanding and adherence
* Decrease unnecessary phone calls related to side effects
* Support healthcare professionals by reducing education workload
* Increase patient safety and satisfaction

---

## 📊 Evaluation Approach

The system is designed to be evaluated using:

* QR code usage rates
* Patient satisfaction surveys
* Medication error observations
* Reduction in side-effect-related phone calls
